Hacking projects

The project submission period is open!

Submit your project proposal

The submission period is Monday 27 February 2023 - Tuesday 11 April 2023 (16:00 CEST). Note that the closing date has been extended by one day due to Easter Monday. Each project can have a maximum of three project leads.

Project submissions need to cover the following points which will also be used for project review by the BioHackathon Programme Committee:

  • Scope and vision of your project,
  • Alignment with ELIXIR efforts and beyond,
  • Feasibility of your proposal.

We welcome new participants as well as industry engagement.
